# where tf did i park

for the chaotic parking lot that has no mercy.

Save your parking spot with GPS + a photo. Find your way back via an AR beacon on your camera feed. Your spot lives in the cloud — clearing your browser or switching phones won't lose it.

## how it works

1. **park** - the app locks your GPS coordinates, compass heading, and an optional photo of your surroundings
2. **find** - point your camera around and follow the acid-green laser beam straight to your vehicle
3. **done** - when you're within 10 meters it turns gold and tells you you're there

## features

- magic link login - no passwords, just your email
- AR beacon drawn over your live camera feed
- compass + gyroscope for real-time directional guidance
- photo capture so you have a visual reference
- works after a phone wipe, browser clear, or reinstall — everything is in the cloud
- installable as a PWA - add to home screen for full-screen mode

## built for

open parking grounds with no sections, no landmarks, no mercy. hundreds of vehicles crammed into a sandy field. you know the one. thanks srm rmp.
